Why This Ride Matters

Why This Ride Matters

Men's health remains one of the most overlooked health issues in the world

Too many men struggle alone. Too many families lose fathers, sons, brothers and friends.

The Distinguished Gentleman's Ride exists to change that — by raising funds, awareness and starting the conversations that men often find hardest to have.

1 in 7

Men will be diagnosed with prostate cancer.

Every Minute

A man dies by suicide worldwide.

How You Can Support the Ride

This event only happens because people step forward to help

Ride

Register your motorcycle, dress the part, raise awareness for men's health.

Donate

Every donation goes towards the Movember Foundation's work in prostate cancer research and suicide prevention.

Volunteer

Help us manage riders, support fundraising, and keep the event running smoothly.

Sponsor

Local businesses can play a vital role by supporting one of the region's most visible charity events.

“The Distinguished Gentleman's Ride is about more than motorcycles. It's about community, friendship and looking out for one another.”

Every pound raised helps fund vital research, support services and life-saving initiatives for men facing prostate cancer or mental health challenges.
